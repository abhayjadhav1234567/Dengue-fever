DENGUE AWARENESS

Community Engagement Project - Prevention & Control

Project Aim

To increase awareness about dengue fever, its prevention and control measures in the community to reduce the incidence of dengue cases.

Objectives

Education & Awareness

Ensure the local community knows about causes and symptoms of dengue fever.

Preventive Measures

Promote preventive measures: mosquito control, eliminating breeding sites, personal protection.

Community Collaboration

Collaborate with local health authorities and organize awareness & cleaning drives.

Impact Assessment

Assess impact of the awareness program on community knowledge and behaviour.

Expected Outcomes

Improved community knowledge about dengue and its prevention.

Reduced mosquito breeding sites through clean-up drives.

Enhanced collaboration between community and local authorities.

Decrease in number of dengue cases reported.

Improved personal protection (repellents, clothing, screens).

Increased participation in anti-larvae campaigns.

Dengue Prevention Measures

⚠️ Protect Yourself from Dengue ⚠️

Follow these 10 essential prevention tips to avoid dengue infection

1. Eliminate Standing Water

Remove, empty, or cover any items that can hold water where mosquitoes breed.

2. Use Mosquito Repellent

Apply repellents containing DEET, picaridin, or oil of lemon eucalyptus.

3. Wear Protective Clothing

Wear long-sleeved shirts, long pants, socks, and shoes to cover exposed skin.

4. Install Window Screens

Use screens on windows and doors to keep mosquitoes outside.

5. Use Mosquito Nets

Sleep under mosquito nets, especially during daytime.

6. Clean Water Containers

Regularly scrub and clean water storage containers.

7. Support Fogging

Cooperate with local authorities during fogging operations.

8. Avoid Peak Mosquito Hours

Mosquitoes bite in early morning and late afternoon.

9. Community Clean-up

Join community efforts to remove mosquito breeding sites.

10. Seek Early Treatment

If you experience dengue symptoms, seek medical attention immediately.
