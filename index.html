Awareness & Prevention of Dengue Fever

Community Engagement Project Proposal • Medical Sector

Move your mouse… the swarm follows.

See Plan Objectives

Aim

To increase awareness about dengue fever, its prevention and control measures in the community, to reduce the incidence of dengue cases.

Objectives

Ensure the local community knows causes and symptoms of dengue fever.
Promote prevention: mosquito control, eliminating breeding sites, personal protection.
Collaborate with local health authorities; organize awareness & cleaning drives.
Assess impact on community knowledge and behaviour.

Expected Outcomes

Improved community knowledge about dengue and its prevention.
Reduced breeding sites via clean-up & waste management.
Enhanced collaboration between community and local authorities.
Decrease in dengue cases during and after the project.
Better personal protection (repellents, clothing, screens).
Higher participation in anti-larvae campaigns and clean-up drives.

Plan of Execution

  1. Week 1 — Introduce project goals and objectives.
  2. Week 2 — Assemble team and assign roles.
  3. Week 3 — Use social media platforms for awareness.
  4. Week 4 — Organize community clean-up events.
  5. Week 5 — Assess project impact and effectiveness.
  6. Week 6 — Plan for sustained awareness efforts.
  7. Week 7 — Document outcomes and gather feedback.

Place & Local Authority Involvement

Place: Girls Hostel, Ghulewadi, Sangamne

  • Local health department to provide expert resources and data.
  • Municipal corporation to assist in waste disposal and cleaning drives.
