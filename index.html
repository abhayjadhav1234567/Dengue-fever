Dengue Prevention

  • Eliminate standing water where mosquitoes breed
  • Use mosquito repellent containing DEET
  • Wear long-sleeved shirts and long pants
  • Install window and door screens
  • Use mosquito nets while sleeping

Dengue Symptoms

  • High fever (104°F/40°C)
  • Severe headache
  • Pain behind the eyes
  • Muscle and joint pain
  • Nausea and vomiting

Essential Prevention Measures

Remove Standing Water

Regularly empty and clean containers that hold water, such as buckets, flower pots, and bird baths.

Use Mosquito Repellent

Apply EPA-approved repellents containing DEET, picaridin, or oil of lemon eucalyptus.

Protective Clothing

Wear long-sleeved shirts, long pants, socks, and shoes to cover exposed skin.

Window Screens

Install or repair window and door screens to keep mosquitoes outside.
